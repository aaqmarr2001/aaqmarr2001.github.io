Introduction

Bangladesh is a huge delta region formed to the north of the Bay of Bengal, with India surrounding all 3 other borders.

1971 saw Bangladesh's independence war, which was in response to the atrocities and animosity of Pakistani troops in Bangladeshi land.

Bangladesh is the only country to have ever fought for its mother language - Bengali.
